Purpose

From the way we wrap our burgers to how we flip the patties and source the potatoes, there’s a method to everything we do at Taste Lounge.

THE TASTE LOUNGE WAY

Real Food, Really Handmade.

When we say our food is handmade, we mean it. Our beef is hand-pattied and fries are sliced fresh in-store. Each morning, our skilled team tears lettuce by hand, chops onions, slices peppers and prepares every ingredient carefully to give you the highest quality meals.

OUR PREPARATION

The Why Behind The Soup

A lot of thought and craft goes into our Soup. We carefully source our veggies and hand-cut them in-store. Using 100% refined palmoil oil, we fry them through a three-step process that gives Taste Lounge soup their signature crispness, flavor, and unforgettable taste.
